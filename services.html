Get Started



01

To begin we will connect by phone call or video conference to have a 15 minute consultation call to chat about your business and vision.

02

After the discovery call you will receive a proposal that will include the project outline, list of deliverables & a contract. Once you approve the proposal, sign the contract and pay the 50% down payment, you'll receive an in-depth questionnaire to provide us with enough information to create the brand or logo that best matches your vision.

03

This is where we present the strategy & design and get your feedback. If you're not satisfied we will go back into the revision process until we've reached the perfect final result.

04

With everything completed we will collaboratively review it once more to make sure everything is perfect. When the remaining balance is paid you will receive your final files. Then it's time to launch.
